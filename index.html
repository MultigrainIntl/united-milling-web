Our story

A century on the land shapes how we farm today.

For more than 100 years, our family has worked the prairie through changing seasons, changing markets, and generations of progress. That history has taught us to think beyond a single crop year.

We believe good farming begins with respect—for the land, for the crop, and for the people who depend on it. United Milling carries that tradition forward with practical knowledge, modern production practices, and a long-term view of every field we farm.
